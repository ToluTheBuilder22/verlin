Admission Requirement

Undergraduate Admission

  • Five credit passes in O'Level (WAEC/NECO) including English and Mathematics
  • A very good UTME Score and Post-UTME performance
  • Valid birth certificate and passport photograph

Direct Entry Admission

  • Minimum of Upper Credit in OND, NCE.
  • Five O'Level credit passes including English and Mathematics
  • Transcript from previous institution

Postgraduate Admission

  • Bachelor's degree (Minimum Second Class Lower Division) from a recognized Univeristy.
  • NYSC discharge or exemption certificate
  • Academic transcript and reference letters.

Admission Process

Undergraduate

  1. Visit the online admission portal.
  2. Create an applicant profile.
  3. Fill out the application form and upload required document.
  4. Print the acknowledgement slip and await screening schedule.

Direct Entry

  1. Follow the undergraduate application steps.
  2. Select "Direct Entry" as your mode of entry
  3. Upload credentials.

Postgraduate

  1. Visit the Postgraduate admission portal.
  2. Create an account and choose your desired program.
  3. Upload academic transcript,certificate and reference letter.
